Incredible Marathons Around the World

From magical to chaotic

Running a marathon can be about more than just miles — it can be about magic, history, breathtaking views, and unforgettable experiences. From whimsical routes through Disney parks to grueling climbs up ancient walls, these five incredible marathons each offer something unique for runners seeking challenge, fun, or inspiration. Whether you’re chasing a personal best or soaking up the scenery, here are some races that are worth adding to your bucket list.

Breathtaking Marathons

Honolulu Marathon – Hawaii, USA

Starting before sunrise to beat the tropical heat, this scenic marathon hugs the coast of Oahu. From the starting line at Ala Moana Beach Park to the finish at Kapiolani Park, runners pass through postcard-perfect spots like Waikiki Beach, Diamond Head, and Koko Head Crater. With no time limit, it’s one of the most welcoming marathons for runners of all experience levels — whether you're racing or simply enjoying the view.

Great Wall Marathon – China

One of the world’s most physically demanding races, the Great Wall Marathon takes place on the historic Huangyaguan section of the Great Wall. While you won’t run the entire 13,000-mile structure, the course still includes more than 5,000 rugged steps and sharp inclines. Runners are rewarded with jaw-dropping views of surrounding mountains and the chance to tread one of humanity’s greatest engineering feats.

Berlin Marathon – Germany

Our Home Race! No list would be complete without highlighting our own local gem! Famous for its fast, flat course, Berlin has earned its place as one of the six World Marathon Majors. Known for electric energy and a historic route past landmarks like the Brandenburg Gate and the Reichstag, this is the place to push for a personal best. In fact, the current men’s world record was set here in 2022. Don’t leave without grabbing a photo at the East Side Gallery — it's Berlin in a nutshell. Mark your calendars — this one is worth training for!

The querky ones

Walt Disney World Marathon – Florida, USA

Magic meets mileage at this one-of-a-kind marathon through all four iconic Disney parks — Magic Kingdom, EPCOT, Hollywood Studios, and Animal Kingdom. With thousands of runners in costume, DJs, live bands, and beloved characters cheering you on, the event feels more like a celebration than a competition. Fireworks mark the start, and the enchanting finish makes this race a fairytale experience for runners of all ages.

The Vortex Race – Switzerland

Held in Lausanne and organized by students from EPFL (École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne), this relatively new race dares participants to conquer the intense Vortex ramp. Open to all — students or not — the race combines adrenaline with purpose. Each year, proceeds go to a charitable cause, and in 2024, the focus is on Loisirs pour Tous, a foundation promoting inclusive access to sports for people with disabilities.
